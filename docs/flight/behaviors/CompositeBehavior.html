Classpublic dynamic class CompositeBehavior
InheritanceCompositeBehavior flash.utils.Proxy
Implements IBehavior, flash.events.IEventDispatcher

A dynamic proxy for compositing multiple behaviors. The CompositeBehavior may be iterated through just as an Array might be.
